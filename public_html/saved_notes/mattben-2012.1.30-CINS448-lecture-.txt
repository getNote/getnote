<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Example</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">if there was a fire we would expect to
lose 50% of our assets (exposure factor) which are worth $1 million.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in"><br>
</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">The annualized rate of occurrence is
1/10 (once in 10 years)</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">($1M x 50% = $5000K) x 1/10 = $50K</p>

<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Map Safeguards to Risks</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">What can be done? (risk mitigation)<br></p>
<ol>
	<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Select the appropriate safeguards</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Map safeguards and vulnerabilities
	to all related threats</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Determine if the safeguard will
	mitigate the risk to an acceptable level</p>
	<p style="margin-bottom: 0in"></p>
	<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Threat  ← Vulnerability ←
	Safeguard</p></li></ol>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Calculate Gross Risk Reduction Benefit
(how much will we save in the long run)</p>
<ol>
	<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Calculate how much each threat is
	going to cost us</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Implement the safeguard, calculate
	the new threat cost <font size="2">(overall cost of threats annual
	with safeguard)</font></p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Cost from threat – cost with
	safeguard in place = gross risk reduction benefit</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Gross risk reduction benefit –
	safeguard's annual average cost = Net Annual Benefit</p></li></ol>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Residual Risk</p>
<ol>
	<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">The chance of threats after
	implementation of the safeguard</p>
</li></ol>

<ol>
	<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Intended to lower the cost of
	overprotecting all data</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Protecting mechanisms are
	implemented where they are needed most, and less costly controls can
	be put in place for non-critical information</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Implement biometric system</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Classified information</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Implement username / password
	system</p>
</li></ol>
