<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Safeguard</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">-A risk-reducing measure that acts to:</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">&nbsp; Detect</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">&nbsp; Prevent</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">&nbsp; Or minimize loss</p>

</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Uncertainty</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">The degree (0.0 – 100%) to which
there is less than complete confidence in the value of any element of
the risk assessment</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in"><br>
</p>

(ARO) and the exposure factor (EF)</p>

<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">-Once a safeguard is selected how much

<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">-TCO (Total Cost of Ownership) &lt; ALE
