<ol>

	keys to the kingdom)</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Responsibilities include:</p>
	<ol>

<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Security Analyst</p>

<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">A Security Program</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Policy</p>
<ol>
	<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">A high level statement of
	enterprise beliefs, goals, and objectives and the general means for
	their attainment</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Senior management is directly
	involved its creation</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">does not include list of
	technologies to use</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">it does not delve into specifics
	(high level not low level)</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Establish an Information Security
	Policy</p>
	<ol>
		<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">All information, regardless of
		the form of format, that is created or used … (example)</p></li></ol></li></ol>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Procedures</p>
<ol>
	<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Spell out the step-by-step process
	of how the policy will actually be implemented in the environment</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Example:</p>
	<ol>
		<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">“All users of McAfee anti-virus
		software will have anti-virus signature files ...”</p></li></ol></li></ol>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Standards</p>
<ol>
	<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">A specific product of mechanism
	that is selected for universal use</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Example:</p>
	<ol>
		<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">“Custodians of information
		processing system must use McAfee.. “</p></li></ol></li></ol>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Baseline</p>
<ol>
	<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">How to implement a security
	package to ensure that it is done in a consistent level of security
	through out the organization</p>
	</li><li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Example”</p>
	<ol>
		<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0in">“The values to set the
		parameters in the McAfee anti-virus software will be ...”</p></li></ol></li></ol>
